In 2018, I came up with the idea of creating something that would put an end to the era of losing to the bookmaker.
 I started backwards. 
Instead of creating a model first, I created mathematical formulas. 
I spent three years creating formulas, then arranging them to be coherent and logical for the next two years. 
So, it's 2023. 
I started building a model. 
My first attempts yielded nothing. 
My mind went blank until 2025, when something struck me and I gained a few clues, thanks to which in December 2025 and January 2026, a model called Karasu v2 was created. It's a universal model. 
I tested it on the largest variation, which is football, but there are 14 other sports. 
I don't want to go into detail, but there are basketball, volleyball, tennis, hockey, baseball, and others. 
So, in conclusion, the model has shown its value in football and I hope that it will be available to everyone someday.
